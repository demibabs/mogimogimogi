Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data mogibot collects and how it is used:

1. What We Collect

  • Discord user IDs, server IDs, and game-related stats for leaderboard and tracking features.
  • No sensitive personal information (such as email, password, or real name) is collected or stored.

2. How We Use Data

  • Data is used solely for providing bot features like leaderboards, stats, and server management.
  • Data is not shared with third parties except as required by law or Discord's policies.

3. Data Removal

  • Server owners may request deletion of their server's data by removing the bot and contacting the developer.
  • Individual users may request removal of their data by contacting the developer.
