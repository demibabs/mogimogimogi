lots of numbers

with /stats, /rank-stats and /notables, see detailed data from all of your (or any player's) matches, along with highlights and lowlights. you can also use the filters to break them down by time and format :)

player comparisons

with /head-to-head, you can compare any two players across shared mogis, and with /leaderboard, view server-wide mmr rankings. settle the score with your friends and rivals >:)

personalized cards

with /customize, add your favorite track, character and vehicle to your personal cards. your stats are unique to you, so you can make them look unique as well :D
