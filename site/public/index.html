individual stats

with /stats, /rank-stats and /notables, you get detailed numbers about your performances, along with recaps of your best and worst mogis. you can also use the filters to break them down by time and format.

player comparisons

with /head-to-head, you can compare any two players across shared mogis, and with /leaderboard, you can compare mmr across your entire server. settle the score with your friends and rivals!

personalized cards

with /customize, add your favorite track, character and vehicle to your personal cards. your stats are unique to you, so you can make them look unique as well :)
