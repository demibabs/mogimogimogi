how it works

i have a large amount of match tables stored in a database.

when you run a command, it checks the database for your tables, while also automatically adding any matches you've played that aren't there yet. it then uses them to calculate the stats you asked for.

there are a few advantages to this approach. the main one is that this bot is able to provide more detailed stats than you can get elsewhere (along with head to heads), with better filtering as well. being able to calculate directly from full table data gives me a lot of freedom.

but as with everything, there are tradeoffs. i don't think this bot is that slow, but it's definitely slower than other stats bots, especially if you've played a lot of mogis, and especially if you're trying to get the stats of someone who's not yet in my database.

so, this isn't meant to be a replacement for other similar bots. just something that can provide interesting data for people who are nerds about game stats like me :)
